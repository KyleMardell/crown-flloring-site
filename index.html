Crown Carpets and Flooring has been providing high quality flooring solutions since 2019. Our dedication to quality and customer satisfaction is what sets us apart in West Yorkshire. Whether you're looking to refresh a single room or undertake a large-scale commercial project, we have the expertise to deliver exceptional results.

Our team specializes in a comprehensive range of flooring services, including fitting and removal for both commercial and domestic applications. We handle all types of flooring:

  • Carpet
  • Hardwood
  • Laminate
  • Vinyl
  • Tile
  • Artificial Grass

Crown Carpets and Flooring proudly serves the entire West Yorkshire region, with a primary base in Leeds. We are committed to providing timely and efficient services to all our clients within this area.

Customer satisfaction is at the heart of what we do. With over 100 positive reviews on Facebook and numerous testimonials, we have built a reputation for reliability, quality, and excellent customer service. Our clients appreciate our personable approach and our commitment to communication throughout each project.
